📊 SafeDose Database Design

MongoDB Collections & Schema Architecture

Database Overview

SafeDose uses MongoDB as its primary database, with collections organized around core entities:

  • User: Authentication and identity management (role-based: admin, patient, caregiver)
  • Patient: Clinical records and patient profiles with medication and adherence tracking
  • CaregiverProfile: Extended caregiver information with professional credentials
  • Assignment: Links caregivers to patients with role-based access control
  • CaregiverRequest: Manages patient-caregiver linking requests and invitations
  • Medication: Drug information, dosage, schedule, and taken-dose tracking per patient
  • DailyAdherence: Daily snapshots of medication adherence with detailed per-medication breakdown

The schema supports real-time monitoring, historical analytics, and role-based data access across the SafeDose platform.
