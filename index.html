SafeDose

Medication Safety and Adherence Tracker

Mission

Help patients take the right medication at the right time, safely.

Vision

Build a connected medication-care ecosystem for patients, caregivers, and healthcare teams.

Ravindar (UI/Docs) • Jahnavi (Backend/Auth) • Doyen (DB/DevOps)

Slide 2

The Problem

  • Medication non-adherence remains a major healthcare risk.
  • Reminders, safety checks, and caregiver visibility are fragmented across tools.
  • Families and care teams need shared, real-time medication context.
This is not only a UX issue; it is a system efficiency and patient safety issue.
Slide 3

The Opportunity

  • Growing chronic care demand increases medication complexity.
  • Digital health adoption is accelerating in home-care settings.
  • There is room for integrated adherence + safety + collaboration.
Slide 4

Solution + Architecture

Next.js Frontend Node.js + Express APIs MongoDB Data Layer Google Gemini (Chat + TTS) OpenFDA Integration Push + Email Reminders Docker + Kubernetes + GCP-ready

Modular architecture for scalability and role-based security boundaries.
