Problem & Goal

OracleLens addresses the challenge of making computational resources tradable in a decentralized way, while preserving performance and trust. Our goal was to enable providers to stake and sell GPU/CPU resources and allow buyers to execute ML workloads efficiently across distributed compute nodes.

Marketplace Trust Secure staking and trading across participants.
Local Operation Run, test, and validate without external dependency overhead.
ML Efficiency Preserve strong model behavior across distributed compute.

Final Scope Delivered

1. Full local functionality without relying on external frameworks/libraries.
2. Advanced smart contracts for staking, buying, and selling GPU/CPU compute.
3. Improved decentralized ML execution for near unified-GPU behavior.
4. Final end-to-end testing checklist completed for full validation.
